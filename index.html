About Me

I'm a website designer/front-end developer with full stack skills and a strong project management and organizational change management background. I am passionate about creating clean, modern websites that delight end users and drive business results.


When I'm not creating web applications, I enjoy photography and play the alto saxophone. I also love writing modern haiku and studying screenwriting. Above all, nothing brings me more joy than spending time with my husband and son in the beautiful New Hampshire outdoors.
